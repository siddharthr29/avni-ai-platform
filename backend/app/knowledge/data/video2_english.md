### TAB 5: W3H (What, When, Who, How, Forms to be Scheduled)

The next tab here is the **W3H** — **What, When, Who, How, Forms to be Scheduled, and Notes**.

#### WHAT — "The Heart of Our Scoping Documents"

So the **What** tab — **this is the heart of our scoping documents**. So this gives us a walkthrough of how your field workflow is.

So the What section covers **what kind of activities are done**. Like for this Phulwari program:
1. They first do a **Child Registration**
2. Then they **Enroll** the child into the Phulwari program
3. Then they have **Anthropometry Assessment**
4. They take their **Daily Attendance**
5. And then there's a **Child Exit**

So this is What — what are the activities you do?

#### WHEN

**When** is this done? Like when do you register this child? So for them — whenever they identify a child in their home visit or Anganwadi center — they identify a child between the age of six months to three years — they are going to register that child. After that registration, what they are going to do — they are going to enroll that child in the Phulwari program. So same day — as soon as they register, they enroll.

And then they do an Anthropometry Assessment on a **monthly basis**. They take their height and weight, check their BMI, and they identify if the child is SAM child, MAM child, and then accordingly there is a referral requirement — they refer the child for the referral.

#### WHO

Similarly, the **Who** tab gives us who is going to do this detail — who is going to fill these details or do this intervention on the field. Like for example, the Phulwari workers/users — they are going to register the child. All these interventions are going to be done by — some of them are going to be done by the Phulwari, some both Phulwari and Supervisor can do — so you can mention that in the Who section.

#### HOW

**How** section covers how are they going to do it — are they going to use the **Avni mobile application**, or are they going to use our **Avni web app** for entering the details.

#### FORMS TO BE SCHEDULED

**Forms to be Scheduled** — this step gives us an idea about if there is a fixed period in which this intervention happens. Like for example, Child Anthropometry — it happens **first of every month**. So accordingly we can schedule the form — the visits — for this particular form. So this gives us an idea about that.
